A flow of information

Before diving into programming with Paragon, let us introduce (or: recap) the concept of information flow.

Computer security is about the protection of information. For many decades research in security has focussed on regulating access to information. Parallel, but much less in the spotlight, researchers have been addressing the question of how to regulate what happens to information once a process has gained access to that information.

Chalmers University, Göteborg, Sweden
